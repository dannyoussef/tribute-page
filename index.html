Kobe Bryant

Timeline

  • 1978 - Born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  • 1995 - Leads high school team to their first state championship in 53 years
  • 1996 - Declares for the NBA draft right out of high school, gets selected by the Charlotte Hornets with the 13th pick but traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  • 2000 - Wins first NBA championship
  • 2006 - Scores a career-high of 81 points, the second most points scored in a single game
  • 2010 - Wins the fifth and final NBA championship of his career
  • 2012 - Wins second gold medal as a member of the U.S national basketball team
  • 2015 - Announces retirement, composes a poem entitled "Dear Basketball" as a final farewell to the sport he loved
  • 2018 - Wins the Academy Award for Best Animated Short Film for his Dear Basketball film
  • 2020 - Dies in a helicopter crash along with his daughter, Gianna, at the age of 41 in Calabasas, California

"I admired him because of his passion, you rarely see someone who is looking and trying to improve each and every day, not just in sports, but as a parent, as a husband."

- Michael Jordan, 6x NBA Champion
